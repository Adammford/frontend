Pad Thai

Description:

This is a traditional pad thai recipe used by a friend's mother. You can use chicken, pork, beef, tofu, or a combination. You may want to start with less pepper and work your way up.

Ingredients:

  • Package of rice noodles
  • Butter
  • Chicken breasts halves, cut into pieces
  • Vegetable oil
  • Eggs
  • White sugar
  • Fish sauce
  • White wine vinegar
  • Crushed red pepper
  • Bean sprouts
  • Green onions
  • Crushed peanuts
  • Lemon, wedges

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Soak rice noodles in cold water until soft, drain and set aside.
  3. Saute chicken til brown.
  4. Heat oil in pan, cook eggs until firm.
  5. Add noodles, sugar, fish sauce, vinegar, and red pepper.
  6. Stir in bean sprouts.
  7. Serve topped with green onions, crushed peanuts, and lime wedge.
